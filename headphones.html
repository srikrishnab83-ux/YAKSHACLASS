🎧 Headphone Buying Guide

🧠 ANC vs Normal

ANC Headphones block background noise. Best for travel and focus.

Normal Headphones are cheaper and better for awareness while walking.

🔋 Battery Life

Always choose 20+ hours for wireless. For gaming, wired is better for zero latency.

🎙️ How Much Mic Quality Do You Need?

  • Basic Mic: Calls and meetings.
  • ENC Mic: Recommended for YouTube and online classes.
  • Boom Mic: Best for streaming, gaming and podcasts.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Which headphone is best for YouTube creators?

For beginners, the boAt Rockerz 550 is an excellent choice. Professional creators should consider Sony WH-1000XM5 or Sony WH-CH720N for better audio monitoring.

Is ANC necessary for video editing?

Not mandatory, but ANC helps you hear audio details clearly without background noise, especially in noisy environments.

Should I buy wireless or wired?

Wireless for convenience and music. Wired for gaming and zero-latency video editing.

Are AirPods Max worth buying?

Yes for Apple users. They offer excellent sound, Spatial Audio and seamless integration, but are expensive.

How much battery life should I buy?

At least 20 hours. If you travel, 30-40 hours is recommended.

Do I need a gaming headset for streaming?

If you stream games, yes. A boom mic and low latency are crucial. Otherwise normal headphones with ENC mic work.

Which headphone is best under ₹5000?

The boAt Rockerz 550, JBL Tune 510BT and Noise 200 are among the best options in this price range.

What driver size is best?

40mm-50mm drivers provide good bass. Larger drivers are better for music, smaller for clarity.
