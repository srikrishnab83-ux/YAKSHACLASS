📱 Mobile Accessories Buying Guide

🎥 Gimbal vs Tripod

Tripod - Best for static shots, talking head videos, cheap.

Gimbal - Best for walking shots, smooth cinematic video. Costs more.

🎤 Wired vs Wireless Mic

Wired Lavalier - Cheap, reliable, no battery needed.

Wireless Mic - Freedom to move, better for outdoor. Expensive.

💡 Lighting Priority

  • ₹1000: Clip-on LED light
  • ₹4000: 10-12" Ring Light
  • ₹8000: 18" Ring Light + Softbox

🎬 Best Kit by Budget

  • 📱 Under ₹2000 → Tripod + Boya Mic
  • 🎥 Under ₹7000 → Ring Light + Tripod + Mic
  • 🚀 Under ₹20000 → Gimbal + Ring Light + Wireless Mic

🚀 Tips for Mobile Creators

🔊 Audio First

Bad video is ok. Bad audio is not. Buy a mic before anything else.

💡 Light is Everything

A ₹3000 ring light will make your phone footage look like ₹1 lakh camera.

🧠 Stabilize

Shaky video looks amateur. Use tripod or gimbal for smooth shots.

🔋 Power

Carry 20000mAh powerbank. Mobile shoots drain battery fast.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can I shoot YouTube videos with just my phone?

Yes. Add a tripod, mic and ring light and your phone can produce pro quality videos.

Do I need a gimbal for YouTube?

Only if you shoot walking/talking videos. For desk videos, tripod is enough.

Which mic is best for mobile?

Boya BY-M1 for wired. Rode Wireless Go II for wireless. Both work great with phones.

What size ring light should I buy?

10" for desk. 18" for full body. 18" is most popular for YouTubers.

Is iPhone or Android better for accessories?

Both work. Most accessories are universal. iPhone has better video, Android has more options.
